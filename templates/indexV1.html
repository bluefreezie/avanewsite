Milestone History

  • March 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our 'Pilot Program' marked our first steps into the realm of Single-Family Residential (SFR) properties, with the acquisition of 10 properties amounting to 20 units. It was our first chance to test our innovative investment thesis, serving not only to expand our portfolio but also to gain invaluable insights into the dynamics of the market.

  • March 2021

    Fund I

    Successfully raising $30M in equity marked a significant achievement, leading to the deployment of investments into approximately 100 homes or 200 units across Ontario, an initiative known as "Fund I". Further underlining the confidence in this approach, a commitment of $5M was made by the sponsor. Additionally, "Fund I" attracted around $70M in acquisition financing, enabling the funding of acquisitions and capital expenditures. This strategic financial leveraging illustrates the ability to identify and capitalize on opportunities within the Ontario residential real estate market.

  • December 2021

    Fund I Financing

    Approximately $80M was secured in term financing, a pivotal move designed to refinance the acquisition debt associated with "Fund I". This proactive step not only solidified financial stability, but also set the stage for future growth and opportunity within the Ontario residential real estate sector.

  • February 2022

    Fund II

    $30M in equity was raised to facilitate the acquisition of a portfolio of 228 Single Family Residential (SFR) properties and 311 units across Ontario, collectively identified as "Fund II". This transaction, valued at $80M, marks a notable progression in the Ontario real estate sector. Additionally, a sponsor commitment of $5M was secured, underlining the strategic approach towards these investments.

  • July 2022

    Ukrainian Housing

    In conjunction with the launch of "Fund II", a Ukrainian Aid Program was introduced, offering rent-free housing options to Ukrainian families displaced to Canada. This altruistic initiative further exemplifies a commitment to social responsibility within the broader investment strategy.

  • May 2023

    Fund II Second Close

    An additional equity raise of up to $20M is being targeted, intended to be deployed within the "Fund II" investment strategy. This represents an ongoing commitment to maximize the investment potential of the Ontario residential real estate market.

  • Fall 2023

    Fund III

    Aiming to shape the future of Canadian real estate, plans are underway to raise approximately $300M in equity during 2022/2023. This ambitious initiative, known as "Fund III", targets the acquisition of a diverse mix of both developmental and stabilized Single Family Residential (SFR) assets across Canada. The goal is not only to enhance the investment portfolio, but also to create a multitude of opportunities for growth and prosperity

Corey Hawtin

Excutive Chairman

Corey is the founder of Avanew. He brings considerable breadth and depth of professional experience to the management and oversight of the company.

In addition to Core, Corey has founded and successfully grown three affiliate construction companies including Mazenga Building Group, one of Toronto’s most exclusive commercial and residential builders. Corey’s success has been driven by his passion for process driven management and value optimization of Core’s investments.

Corey studied at Guelph University with a major in Finance and Economics.

Adam Marcus

Cheif Executive Officer

Adam has over two decades of experience in originating, evaluating, and executing private equity and capital market transactions. Prior to joining Avanew, Adam was a Strategic M&A Advisor to STS Renewables, a vertically integrated geothermal developer.

Previously Adam was a Managing Director at DRI Capital where he led the transaction origination function and a Managing Director at Macquarie Capita! where he co-led the financial sponsor and leverage finance group. Prior to Macquarie Adam was a Principal at Bank of America in both New York and Toronto. In these roles, Adam developed a wide range of expertise in transaction sourcing, structuring, valuation, diligence, and execution.

Adam holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree in International Business and Finance from McGill University and is a CFA Charterholder..

Shaunt Kalloghlain

President

Shaunt serves as President at Avanew and is responsible for operations, business development, property management, and acquisitions of income-generating residential assets across Canada.

Shaunt has over fifteen years of experience in capital markets, M&A and private equity operations. Prior to joining Avanew, he was a founding Partner at Solon Capital Partners, a West-Africa focused private equity firm. In addition to evaluating investment opportunities and deploying the firm’s capital, while at Solon Shaunt co-founded three of the firm’s operating businesses and served in a variety of operating roles across their portfolio. Prior to that, Shaunt worked as an investment banker at Merrill Lynch where he was focused on M&A and mining industry coverage.

Shaunt holds an Honours in Business Administration from the Richard Ivey School of Business.

Alex Herold

Chief Investment Officer

As Chief Investment Officer, Alex leads the acquisition and underwriting team for Core’s investment portfolio which encompasses asset diligence, transaction structuring and financing, as well as investor relations.

Prior to joining Core, Alex was a Vice President at Forum Equity Partners, where he contributed to advancing the company’s real estate and renewables investments. Alex’s early career was established in investment banking, focusing on M&A, public equity and debt financing: primarily in the natural resources sector. Alex has held investment banking positions in Toronto, London and New York, most recently with Goldman Sachs.

Alex holds an Honors Business Administration degree from The Ivey Business School and a Bachelor of Engineering Science degree from The University of Western Ontario.

Cole Rodness

Director, Asset Management

As Director of Asset Management, Cole is responsible for managing the Avanew Single-Family Rental Fund with a principal focus on driving value and increasing net income for the portfolio. Cole’s other responsibilities include evaluating new acquisitions, cash flow modeling, building and maintaining relationships with financial partners and third-party vendors, and exploring new technology solutions for the portfolio.

Prior to joining Avanew, Cole served as an Associate Director of Investments at Hazelview Investments. His responsibilities included underwriting acquisitions in multiple real estate asset classes across major Canadian markets, asset managing Hazelview’s existing multi-unit residential portfolio in Southwestern Ontario and Atlantic Canada, and served on Hazelview’s Sustainability Sub-Committee.

Cole holds an Honours Business Administration degree from the Ivey Business School at the University of Western Ontario.
