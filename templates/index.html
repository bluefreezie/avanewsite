Avanew
Canada's Leading SFR Asset Manager

Avanew has pioneered the Single Family Rental (SFR) asset class in Canada by assembling the largest portfolio of attainable, institutional-quality rental homes in Ontario. We acquire existing, underdeveloped or underinvested residential real estate assets and complete significant renovations to enhance the livability, convenience, energy efficiency and aesthetic of what's available to Canadians.

Our Impact

Since the inception of the Avanew Homes Ukrainian Aid Program ("AHUAP") in 2022, we have welcomed 20 families into our properties. Nearly all of the newcomers who are of working age have secured employment, and all of the children are enrolled in both school and extracurricular activities. The families have a strong sense of community; with the assistance of our wonderful partners at the Canadian Ukrainian Immigrant Aid Society (CUIAS) and the local Ukrainian communities in our placement cities, they have swiftly adapted to life in Ontario.

Investment Strategies

Development

Redeveloping underutilized properties to uplift the standard of living, densify assets and enhance community value. As older houses are reinvigorated, neighborhoods experience transformation with an increase in safety standards and property values. In Fund 1, Avanew purchased 100 Single Family Homes and converted these into 200 rental units.

Build to Rent

Avanew is bringing the Build-to-Rent industry (BTR) to Canada. Renters’ demands for ground-oriented dwellings are not being met today; the need has never been greater for increased housing stock and rental options across Ontario/Canada while maintaining diverse communities accessible to residents of all income levels.

Stabilization

Purchasing occupied rental properties in high-quality neighbourhoods at attractive yields, ensuring a stable stock of rentable properties.

Adam Marcus

Chief Executive Officer

Adam has over two decades of experience in originating, evaluating, and executing private equity and capital market transactions. Prior to joining Avanew, Adam was a Strategic M&A Advisor to STS Renewables, a vertically integrated geothermal developer.
